# Butler Expert Adapter

Domain-specific LoRA adapter for conversational assistance tasks.

## Capabilities

- Scheduling and calendar management
- Reminder and notification setup
- Weather and news queries
- General information lookup
- Recommendation and suggestion
- Polite, helpful conversation

## Training Data

Fine-tuned on:
- Personal assistant dialogues
- Customer service interactions
- Scheduling conversation logs
- General knowledge Q&A

## Trigger Keywords

High confidence triggers:
- "schedule", "appointment", "meeting", "calendar"
- "remind", "reminder", "notification"
- "weather", "news", "recommend"
- Polite phrases: "help me", "please", "thank you"
